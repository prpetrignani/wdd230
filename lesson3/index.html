Site Name

Verona Chamber Commerce

Site Purpose

The Verona Chamber of Commerce was made to help people achieve their goals. The chamber offers opportunities to connect the user with experts in many fields, and receives updates about initiatives and cultural events related to Verona and its region. But the Verona Chamber of Commerce works also as a showcase where selected professionals and talented young people can offer their knowledge and experience to those who need them.
