Discover the Company

The fonunder, Leam Douglas Petrignani, began taking care of his Grandmother's vegetable garden over the 2003 holiday from school. He learnd by her Grandma how to take care and make grow every sinlge vegetable and fruit.
In April 2005, after gaining enough money from his job, he started his own garden and join the Brentwood farmers market. Since then, He has been expanding the business to new markets, began a made-to-order business and, in 2008, the first Bountiful Food retail store opened.
The crazy initial success of Bountiful is no bigger surprise than to us. We were a serious Fashion / Advertising / Design Company.
Our drinks are sweet, savory and healthy; mostly old-fashioned fruit; some 2-crust lattice, some streusels; always local, seasonal farmer's fruit.
Now our Company is expanding over the cost, reaching out many costumers, making their life at the same time sweeter and healthier.
